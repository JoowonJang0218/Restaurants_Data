About WhyCookIn

WhyCookIn is inspired by the playful mispronunciation of “foreigner” (외국인) in Korean, often heard as “외쿸인.” We embrace that spirit of bridging cultures through aiming to be the largest, most welcoming community for foreigners in Korea. From discovering restaurants and cafés that accommodate diverse dietary needs—vegan, vegetarian, halal, gluten-free, and more— to finding legal support for housing contracts and everyday life, WhyCookIn is your one-stop platform for navigating Korea with confidence. We’re here to help you feel at home, wherever you come from.

Housing & Legal Services

Renting or buying property in a new country can be risky. That’s why WhyCookIn connects you with legal experts who specialize in housing contracts and everyday legal matters.
Avoid scams and handle your next move with confidence, whether you’re new to Korea or making a long-term transition.
